# ADR: Data Management Strategy

## Title
Data Management Strategy

## Status
Accepted

## Motivation
The current monolithic architecture uses a centralized database, which limits scalability and flexibility. A new data management strategy is needed to support the microservices architecture, enabling independent data storage and access for each service.

## Decision Drivers
- Need for independent data management to support service autonomy.
- Requirement for scalability and performance improvements.
- Desire for flexibility in choosing data storage technologies.
